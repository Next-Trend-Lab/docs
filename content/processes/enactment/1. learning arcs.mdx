# Intro to Learning Arcs
Purpose of the **Main Learning Arc** for the `Accra Resource Center` action-learning journey is...

To produce a tech innovation, informed by the unique context of their place and designed from a living systems paradigm, where the experiential learning process itself is the experimentation by which participants embody a potential system state and can use that collective insight to generate a precise intervention.

# Co-Design Plan
### Roles

In this gathering, participants will be introduce to the role they will play throughout the Journey, they are the enablers of the potential within the local place they will work with. 

1. `Community` is the role of the local place, natural environment, people and institutions in which participants will interact with. 
2. `Hub hosts` have a role to be the guides, the individuals which will contain, support and guide the participants through the learning journey. 
3. `Facilitators` are the leading individuals that will introduce the learning activities that will take place throughout the journey. 

### Collaboration
Collaboration will be weaved into the learning journey from the beginning to the end. People will walk communities and share what they have learned to make sense of the current challenges of each community. The learning journey outcome will come from this learning process, where participants will then work on creating and developing a response to those challenges.

- Explaining the concept of regeneration and showing its steps into practice.

Roles

2. Facilitators as enablers of learning, and 
3. Hub hosts as guides and containers.[^1]

Collaboration

- Sharing information and creating a collective understanding of a good community.
